FAQs

What is thE innovate events summit?

The Innovate Events Summit is a tech conference focused on innovation and emerging technologies for the future of events. It brings together industry leaders, experts, and stakeholders to explore, and learn through keynotes, workshops, panels, and networking opportunities.

Who is THE INNOVATE EVENTS SUMMIT for?

The Innovate Events Summit is designed for anyone working in the events or just interested in what the future holds for technology and innovatione in the space. Whether you are a professionals, entrepreneurs, startup founder, stage hand, thought leaders. Whatever your role is, there is something for you as long as you’re looking to learn, network, and share ideas.
